Episode 1: The Art of Minimalism

Published on April 18, 2026

Show Notes

In this episode, we dive deep into the principles of minimalism with renowned designer, Alex Chen. We explore how adopting a "less is more" philosophy can transform not only our physical spaces but also our mental clarity and digital lives.

Topics discussed:

  • The history and evolution of minimalist design.
  • Practical tips for decluttering your home and workspace.
  • Digital minimalism and managing our relationship with technology.
  • The psychological benefits of a minimalist lifestyle.

Guest: Alex Chen

Alex Chen is an award-winning designer and author of the bestselling book, "The Simple Truth." His work has been featured in numerous design publications, and he is a vocal advocate for intentional living.
