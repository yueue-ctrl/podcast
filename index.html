Episodes Hosts About

FORM is a design theory podcast that uses minimalism to ask the questions most designers think but never say out loud.

Episodes

Episode 1: Simplicity as Thinking

In this episode, we invited both our host, design theorist John Maeda, and Japanese graphic designer Kenya Hara to share their thinking about simplicity.

Episode 2: Simplicity as Empowerment

In this episode, we invite British-American designer Jony Ive to talk about how minimalist design can empower people and give them inspiration.

Episode 3: Simplicity in Fashion

In this episode, we bring in two important figures from fashion: Jil Sander and Phoebe Philo, to discuss minimalism in fashion.
